## From the archives

It has been a while since Twitter was bought and I left it behind. What has stuck with me, though, is how quickly Twitter’s quality seemed to unravel.

Accessibility wasn’t the only thing to suffer, but it was the clearest sign that care and maturity had started to disappear from the product. That’s what this month’s archive pick is about: how [accessibility often reflects how mature an organisation really is](https://www.tempertemper.net/blog/accessibility-represents-maturity).
